The dataset includes the several properties of the 115 tractors produced by 
three different brands with their price. 

price: the price of the tractor
horsepower: the horsepower of the tractor
brand: the brand of the tractor
number_of_cylinder: the number of cylinder of the tractor
number_of_clicking: the number of clicking of the tractor on the brand's web page 
number_of_failure: the number of failure of the tractor model since it has been produced
province: the name of the province where the tractor was sold 
warranty_period: the warranty period of the tractor
warranty_period_unit: the unit of the warranty period (year)
age : the age of the tractor
